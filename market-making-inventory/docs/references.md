# References

## Academic Papers

### Foundational Works

1. **Avellaneda, M., & Stoikov, S. (2008)**
   - Title: "High-frequency trading in a limit order book"
   - Journal: Quantitative Finance, 8(3), 217-224
   - DOI: 10.1080/14697680701381228
   - Key contribution: First stochastic control formulation of market making

2. **Guéant, O., Lehalle, C.-A., & Fernandez-Tapia, J. (2013)**
   - Title: "Dealing with the inventory risk: A solution to the market making problem"
   - Journal: Mathematical Finance, 23(3), 517-554
   - DOI: 10.1111/mafi.12017
   - arXiv: 1105.3115
   - Key contribution: Inventory constraints and HJB transformation

3. **Ho, T., & Stoll, H. R. (1981)**
   - Title: "Optimal dealer pricing under transactions and return uncertainty"
   - Journal: Journal of Financial Economics, 9(1), 47-73
   - Key contribution: Early market making model

5. **Guéant, O., & Lehalle, C.-A. (2015)**
